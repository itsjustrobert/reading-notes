FORMS AND JAVASCRIPT EVENTS

Forms

The best known form on the webpage is the search box.
In adittion to allowing users to search, forms also allow the user
to perform other functions online. You will see forms when
registering as a member of a website, when shopping online
and when signing up for newsletters or mailing lists.
